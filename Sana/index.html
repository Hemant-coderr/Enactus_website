About

Project SaNa- Sana abbreviates for SAnitary NApkins and means comfort in Urdu.We produce reusable sanitary napkins, ensuring women hygiene, and generating employment among the underprivileged people. For this we’ve collaborated and covered certain NGOs as well.


In simple language, our project is all about spreading awareness, making reusable sanitary napkins available to those who need it the most and to generate employment among the underprivileged people, especially women. These sanitary napkins will be 75% more biodegradable than the ordinary pads because of the cloth used in it; a water-retaining fabric that prevents leakage. In addition to this, they would be available at cheaper rates than the other substitutes available in the market. Keeping in mind the environmental factors, not just the product, but even its packaging is made up of biodegradable material. Though the journey started with the women in slums and providing them the prerequisites, the project remained successful in expanding its outreach.
